@title[CO2RR]
### Produce propanol from CO2RR

<ol>
<li>
Current industrial production of propanol
    <ul>
    <li>
    ethylene is hydroformylated to propionaldehyde using cobalt or rhodium-based catalysis
    </li>
    <li>
    propionaldehyde is then reduced to propanol
    </li>
    </ul>
